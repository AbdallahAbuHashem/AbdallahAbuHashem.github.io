Organizations Working In Palestine To Consider For Your Donations

One way to help the people in Gaza is donations. This is a list of 4 vetted organizations working on the ground in Gaza with an explanation of what each of them does. The list is based on a post by @palestine5for5 on Instagram

1. Islamic Relief

Main goal: Islamic Relief's main focus is getting food to those who need it most, and treating urgent health needs.

Where will your donation go:

  • Help Islamic Relief teams deliver vital medical supplies to patients and staff.
  • Provide key items; such as medicine, disposables, medical kits and food for all. Donate money to Gaza and help save the lives of many now.

Donating link: https://www.islamic-relief.org.uk/rebuild-gaza/

2. Palestine Children's Relief Fund (PCRF)

Main goal: PCRF has been on the ground providing a variety of direct humanitarian aid programs for thousands of sick and injured children in the enclave.

Where will your donation go:

  • 100% of your donation to PCRF Urgent Gaza Relief will provide free medical and humanitarian aid to injured and sick children in the Gaza Strip based on their needs.

Donating link: https://pcrf1.z2systems.com/np/clients/pcrf1/donation.jsp?campaign=1680

3. UN Relief and Works Agency for Palestine Refugees (UNRWA)

Main goal: Provides support in many forms to Palestinians in need. (Long-term support to Palestinian refugees)

Where will your donation go:

  • Critical healthcare, including psychological support.
  • Food and cash assistance to the poorest of the poor.
  • Construction of desperately needed infrastructure, including schools and shelters.
  • Education for over 250,000 Palestinian refugee students.

Donating link: https://donate.unrwa.org/gaza/~my-donation?_cv=1

4. Medical Aid for Palestinians (MAP)

Main goal: MAP's vision is a future where all Palestinians can access an effective, sustainable and locally-led system of healthcare and the full realisation of their rights to health and dignity.

Where will your donation go:

  • Women and children's health.
  • Emergencies and complex hospital care.
  • Mental health and psychological support.
  • Physical disabilities.

Donating link: https://www.map.org.uk/donate/donate
