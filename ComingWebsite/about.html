A little bit about me

My name is Abdallah AbuHashem. I'm currently a master's student in computer science at Stanford University with a focus on human-computer interaction (HCI). I got into computer science because of my love to building products for people to use. I continue to carry this passion for products and users with me. My design mindset revolves around user interaction and simplicity. I always try to design products in a way that is simple and intuitive to interact with, but without sacrificing complexity.

In addition to UX, I'm also interested in education technology. During my time at Stanford I got to do a minor in education while also working on a research project focused on education technology.

I grew up in Gaza, Palestine, so I speak Arabic fluently as well as English.
